At first sight a commodity presented itself as a complex of two things, use-value and exchange-value. Later we saw that labour, so far as it is expressed in value, no longer possesses the same characteristics that belong to it as a creator of use-values. I was the first to point out critically this twofold nature of the labour contained in commodities. Since this point is the pivot on which a clear comprehension of political economy turns, we must examine it more closely.
