In direct contrast with the coarse materiality of their substance, not an atom of matter enters into the objectivity of commodities as values. Turn and examine a single commodity by itself as we will, yet in so far as it remains an object of value it seems impossible to grasp it. Commodities possess value-objectivity only in so far as they are expressions of the same social substance, human labour; their value-character therefore appears only in the social relation of commodity to commodity.
